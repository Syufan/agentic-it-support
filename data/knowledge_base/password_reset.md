# Password Reset and Account Access

## Okta Password Reset
1. Go to company.okta.com → click "Forgot password".
2. Enter your work email and follow the verification steps.
3. Check spam folder if you do not receive the reset email within 2 minutes.
4. New password must be at least 12 characters and not reused from the last 5 passwords.

## Account Locked Out
- After 5 failed login attempts, your account is locked for 30 minutes.
- Contact IT helpdesk for immediate unlock if you have an urgent meeting.
- Self-service unlock: company.okta.com/unlock-account (requires phone verification).

## MFA Issues
- Lost authenticator device: contact IT immediately — requires identity verification.
- Authenticator out of sync: re-scan the QR code from Okta settings.
- Backup codes: stored in your Okta profile under Security → Extra Verification.

## Escalation Criteria
Escalate to IT if:
- Reset email not received after 10 minutes and spam checked
- Account shows "suspended" rather than "locked"
- User cannot complete phone verification (lost device)
