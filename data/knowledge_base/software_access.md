# Software and System Access Requests

## Requesting New Access
1. Submit a request via the IT portal: it.company.com/access.
2. Specify the system, required permission level, and business justification.
3. Your manager will receive an approval email within 1 business day.
4. Access is provisioned within 4 hours of manager approval.

## Common Systems and Approval Requirements

| System | Approval Required | Provisioning Time |
|--------|------------------|-------------------|
| GitHub (read) | Team lead | 2 hours |
| GitHub (write) | Engineering manager | 4 hours |
| Snowflake (read) | Data team lead | 4 hours |
| Snowflake (write) | VP Engineering | 1 business day |
| Grafana | Team lead | 2 hours |
| AWS console | VP Engineering + Security | 2 business days |
| Salesforce | Sales manager | 4 hours |

## New Employee Access
New employees should receive a standard access package within their first day.
If any standard tools are missing after day 1, contact IT with your employee ID.

## Escalation Criteria
Escalate to IT if:
- Approval was granted but access was not provisioned after the expected time
- You need emergency access outside business hours
- The required system is not listed in the IT portal
